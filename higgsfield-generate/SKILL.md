# Higgsfield Generate Skill

## Overview
AI-powered marketing content generation skill for creating compelling marketing copy, campaigns, and brand-aligned content.

## Capabilities

### Content Generation Modes
- **DTC Advertising**: Direct-to-consumer ad copy and campaigns
- **Brand Kit Mode**: Brand-compliant content generation
- **Avatar Mode**: Audience-specific personalized content
- **Product Mode**: Product-focused marketing copy
- **Setup Mode**: Getting started and onboarding content

## Usage

1. Specify the generation mode
2. Provide brand/product information
3. Define target audience
4. Generate content
5. Refine with feedback

## Output Examples

- Social media posts
- Email campaigns
- Landing page copy
- Product descriptions
- Ad headlines and body copy
