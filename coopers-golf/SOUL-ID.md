# Coopers Golf — Soul ID (Brand Identity)

Produced with the **higgsfield-soul-id** skill. This is the strategic foundation
the 25 logo concepts (see `BRIEF.md`) are built on. Reference brand for tone:
**Druids Golf** — premium-feeling, modern-heritage, "not boring, not loud."

## Brand essence
> Honest, well-made Australian golf wear for people who'd rather be on the course
> than talk about it.

## 1. Mission
Make golf apparel that *looks* premium and *feels* premium, at a price real
golfers can actually justify — engineered for Australian sun, wind and links.

## 2. Vision
Become the everyday golf brand of the Southern Hemisphere: the gear you reach
for first, from the first tee to the 19th hole.

## 3. Values
- **Craft** — a *cooper* is a barrel-maker; we make things built to last, not to hype.
- **Honesty** — fair prices, no inflated "country-club" markup, no nonsense.
- **Easygoing** — golf is social; we live in the post-round culture, not the stiff one.
- **Place** — proudly built for Australian conditions and Australian humour.

## 4. Voice & tone
Dry, warm, confident, low-ego — Australian understatement. We don't shout and we
don't posture. Think a good caddie: knows their stuff, never lectures.
- Say: "Made for the long round." / "Sharp on the course, sharp at the bar."
- Avoid: luxury clichés, exclamation-heavy hype, jargon.

## 5. Visual language
- **Palette:** Heritage Navy `#14213D`, Fairway Green `#1F5135` / Pine `#14402B`,
  Brass/Old Gold `#C8A24B` (metallic accent — the Druids "metal sleeve logo" cue),
  Chalk `#F4EFE3`, Clay Red `#B5402F` (accent), Ink `#15171A`.
- **Type:** heritage serif (crests/clubhouse) + clean modern grotesque (minimal marks);
  optional signature script.
- **Marks:** clean monogram **CG** and club-crest **roundels**, with a metallic accent.
- **Motifs:** flag-in-cup, crossed hickory clubs, dimpled ball, cooper's barrel/cask,
  Southern Cross, eucalyptus, links/coastal horizon.

## 6. Positioning
The **Druids lane, localised for Australia**: sits deliberately between boring
big-box golf basics and the loud hype micro-brands. **Affordable heritage** —
clubhouse-quality look, real-world price.

## Audience
Core: 25–55, weekend & social golfers — value-seeking but style-aware, want to look
sharp without club-shop pricing. Secondary: the lifestyle wearer (golf-as-fashion,
wears the polo off-course too).

## Value proposition
Clubhouse-quality look · real-world price · built for Australian conditions.

---

### How this feeds the logos
- Lean on **monogram + crest** systems (values: craft/heritage) with a **brass metallic** accent.
- The **cooper's barrel** motif (concepts 13–14) is the unique, ownable hook — no other golf brand owns "the cooper's craft."
- Keep at least a few **playful** marks (concepts 24–25) so the range reflects the easygoing, not-stiff voice.
