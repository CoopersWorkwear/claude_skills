# Higgsfield Product Photoshoot Skill

## Overview
Coordinate and plan product photography workflows for e-commerce and marketing.

## Planning Components

- Photoshoot schedule
- Product positioning
- Lighting specifications
- Background requirements
- Props and styling
- Angle recommendations

## Output

- Photoshoot checklist
- Setup diagrams
- Lighting guide
- Styling recommendations
- Post-processing guidelines

## Best Practices

1. Plan shots in advance
2. Test lighting setups
3. Use consistent backgrounds
4. Multiple angles per product
5. High-resolution final images
